WHERE TO BEGIN?

Here are some tips how to start planning birthday party and what to take notice:

  • Budget: How much do you want to spend on the party?
  • Guest list: Who are you inviting to the party?
  • Where: Are you planning to have party at home, restaurant, themepark or somewhere else?
  • When: Is the weekend better than evening after work?
  • Theme: Does your kid have a favourite superhero or some hobbie that suits for theme for the party?
  • What to do: Games, music, quizes, dancing, painting, crafts or something else?

Now it's time to party!
