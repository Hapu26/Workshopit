WHAT TO DO? HOW TO KEEP THE FESTIVE FEELING UP?

Here is a few ideas what to do at the parties.

If you are plannig to keep the parties at home you might have to think some activities, for example:

  • Dance competition
  • Olympics inside or outside
  • Piñata
  • Hide and seek (if the parties are at fall or at winter you might do it in the dark with flashlights!)
  • Quizes
  • Games

You can also keep the parties somewhere else, for example:

  • Bowling centre
  • Indoor sport centre
  • Zoo
  • Amusement park
